The Lion

Inner: The system registers an immediate threat to the personal ego and self-narrative, triggering an absolute requirement to keep their internal identity intact and preserve their personal truth from being compromised or diminished.

Outer: This vulnerability manifests as an unyielding, destructive defense of their reality. They will fiercely confront the source of the threat with overwhelming force, smashing anything in their path, or abruptly sever ties altogether to ensure their narrative remains entirely untouched. Rather than back down or allow a single crack in their stance, they will aggressively double down on a position or walk away mid-interaction, causing observers to view them as deeply self-absorbed, hostile, or hopelessly arrogant.

Influence: Operating as an influence forces an uncompromised, unbending devotion to the self directly into the primary behavior. The active defense mechanism becomes entirely about fighting for their internal alignment; even if the underlying core is desperate to submit or flee, the outward execution remains unyielding, refusing to concede or allow their narrative to be bent by the environment.
